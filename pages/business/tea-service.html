服务概述

Service Overview

技术经济分析（TEA）是评估新技术商业化可行性的重要工具，在学术研究和工业应用中发挥着关键作用。我们提供专业的TEA服务，帮助研究人员和企业全面评估技术的经济性能，为投资决策和技术发展提供科学依据。我们的分析结果已为众多客户在Nature Energy、Joule、Applied Energy等顶级期刊发表研究成果提供了重要支撑。

Techno-Economic Analysis (TEA) is an important tool for assessing the commercial viability of new technologies, playing a key role in academic research and industrial applications. We provide professional TEA services to help researchers and enterprises comprehensively evaluate the economic performance of technologies, providing scientific basis for investment decisions and technology development. Our analysis results have provided important support for numerous clients to publish research results in top journals such as Nature Energy, Joule, and Applied Energy.
